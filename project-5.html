Breather - Mindful Breathing App

Breather is a mindful breathing application designed to help users practice deep breathing exercises. The app features customizable breathing patterns, daily reminders, and a global leaderboard to track progress and encourage mindfulness.

Project Overview

Breather is designed to guide users through various breathing exercises, helping them achieve relaxation and mindfulness. With user-friendly features and a seamless design, it allows users to set daily goals, receive notifications, and track their progress over time.
Feature Highlights:
Customizable Breathing Patterns: Users can choose from a variety of breathing exercises or create their own custom patterns to suit their needs.
Google Login: Easy sign-in and progress tracking with Google authentication.
Notifications: Stay on track with local and push notifications that remind users to practice their breathing exercises.
Global Leaderboard: Motivate users by tracking their progress and comparing it with others globally.
Firebase Integration: Secure and reliable data storage with Firebase, ensuring smooth and real-time updates.
